Blackjack Rules

  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Number cards are worth their number.
  • J, Q, K are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11.
  • The dealer must hit until reaching 17 or more.
  • Blackjack = 21 with the first two cards.
